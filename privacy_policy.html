Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2026-03-28

The MicLabs App (the "App") attaches great importance to user privacy protection. Following the principles of legality, legitimacy, and necessity, we collect, use, and store personal information and protect your privacy and data security. We do not disclose, abuse, or illegally transfer your personal information.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully to understand how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information. By using the App, you confirm that you have fully read, understood, and voluntarily accepted all terms of this Policy.

Article 1: Personal Information We Collect

(I) Information related to registration and login

  • Account identifier from Sign in with Apple: Used for account login and identity display in the App.
  • Account profile information: Nickname and avatar set by you, used for profile display and social interaction in the App.
  • Basic device information: Device model and operating system version, used for compatibility and troubleshooting.

(II) Information collected during use

  • Interaction information: In-app speech and interaction records (used only to optimize services, not for commercial promotion).
  • Usage records: App usage duration, feature modules entered, and operation records (used only for troubleshooting and feature optimization).
  • Permission-related data: If microphone or photo permissions are enabled, access is used only for recording audio or selecting images in features you initiate.

Article 2: How We Use Information

  • To ensure normal App operation, including account verification, device adaptation, troubleshooting, and feature optimization, so you can properly use music communication and interaction services.
  • To enforce rules, identify violations, and handle user complaints, so as to maintain a compliant and healthy environment.
  • Not used for commercial promotion, third-party sharing, or precision marketing.

Article 3: Information Storage and Protection

  • Storage method: Current app data is primarily stored locally on your device (for example, login status, likes, favorites, and reports).
  • Storage period: Data is retained while you use the App. When you delete your account in-app, relevant local data is removed from this device.
  • Protection measures: We apply standard platform protections and local storage safeguards to reduce risks of leakage, tampering, and loss.

Article 4: Information Sharing and Transfer

  • We do not disclose or transfer your personal information to any third party, except in the following circumstances:
    • Where required by laws, regulations, judicial authorities, or administrative authorities.
    • Where necessary to protect your rights or handle violations, we may provide necessary information to relevant authorities (only the part related to the violation, not your full personal information).
  • We do not use your personal information for commercial promotion, nor share it with third parties for profit.

Article 5: Your Rights

  • Right of access: You may view your personal information (such as account profile and avatar) at any time.
  • Right of correction: You may modify editable information such as nickname and avatar.
  • Right of deletion: You may request account deletion; after deletion, all personal information will be permanently removed and cannot be restored.
  • Right of objection: If you believe your personal information is misused or leaked, you may raise an objection to Party A, and Party A will verify and handle it promptly.

Article 6: Supplementary Privacy Notes

  • We do not collect precise location or social relationship data, and do not track user trajectories.
  • We do not sell or share your personal information with any third party for any commercial purpose.
  • If you discover leakage or misuse of personal information, you may contact Party A at any time for prompt verification and handling.

Article 7: Policy Updates and Notice

  • This Privacy Policy may be updated based on legal, regulatory, or operational requirements. After updates, we will notify you through in-app announcements. Continued use of the App means acceptance of the updated Policy.
  • If you do not agree with the updated Policy, you may stop using the App, delete your account, and terminate the rights and obligations between both parties.
